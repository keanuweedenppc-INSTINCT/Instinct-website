Philosophy

The game is
the teacher

Most development programs separate the athlete from the game — isolating skills in drills until they barely resemble basketball. That's not how learning works.

Instinct is built on ecological dynamics and a constraints-led approach. That means shaping the environment so that the right decisions and movements emerge naturally — because the game is asking for them.

Athletes develop perception, decision-making, and creativity alongside the physical skills. Not separately. The game stays whole. The player stays curious.

01

Environment of Joy

Joy isn't a byproduct — it's the mechanism. Athletes who enjoy the process invest in it, and that's where real growth lives.

02

Freedom of Exploration

The right constraints invite the right solutions, without ever telling athletes exactly what to do.

03

Instinct to Compete

Competitiveness trained, not demanded — the trained reaction that shows up when it matters, not just hunger for its own sake.

04

Freedom to Problem-Solve

Athletes who can read the game and find their own answers outlast athletes who can only execute someone else's.

Services

Where I can
help you

1-on-1 Development

Individual sessions tailored to your game profile. We identify what the game is asking of you — and build towards that, using rep-variable practice and real decision-making scenarios.

Small Group Training

2–4 athletes working through shared constraints and game scenarios. The group dynamic creates natural pressure, forcing real reads and real decisions.

Performance Analysis

Game film review through a cognitive and perceptual lens. We look at what you're reading — or missing — before the moment even arrives.

Coaching Education

Workshops and resources for coaches who want to move beyond command-and-drill. Ecological dynamics, constraints-led practice, and building cultures of joy.

Rep Program Design

Curriculum and session design for representative programs — from U12s to Opens. Structured around CLA methodology with year-round progression built in.

Online Consultation

Remote sessions for athletes and coaches wherever you are. We review film, build plans, and work through development priorities together — no court required.

How it works

The process

Profile

We map who you are as an athlete — your movement tendencies, perceptual habits, strengths, and the gaps the game keeps exposing.

Design

A development environment is built around your profile — constraints, tasks, and scenarios that invite the skills you actually need.

Play

You train in conditions that resemble real basketball — not isolated drills. Decision-making and movement co-develop, because they always do.

Review

We look at what's transferring to games, adjust the environment, and keep the challenge in the right zone — not too easy, never arbitrary.
