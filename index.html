FAQ

Good to know

What ages do you train?
Currently U12–U18 for the Willetton Breakfast Club program, and all ages for 1-on-1 and small group sessions.
Can I pause or cancel my subscription?
Yes — subscriptions can be paused for up to 2 weeks at a time, or cancelled anytime through your confirmation email.
Where are sessions held?
Willetton Breakfast Club sessions are held at Willetton's home court. 1-on-1 and small group locations are flexible — get in touch to confirm.
What should my child bring?
Basketball shoes, water bottle, and a willingness to compete. Basketballs are provided.
